About Carter Edge

Carter Edge is a software engineer from wild and wonderful West Virginia, where he lives with one human and one dog. Professionally, he prefers working with python (django, flask, selenium, gunicorn, sqlalchemy) or JavaScript (React, Node, or vanilla), but also has experience with Java (Springboot) and C# (ASP.NET). He has also worked with Docker, AWS, and a wide range of proprietary APIs and platforms.

Unprofessionally, he tinkers with various coding projects, music, writing, and enjoys games of both the table-top and video varieties.
